Mountain Report

Fabulous conditions have prevailed on the upper mountain, with heaps of new snow and freshies everywhere.  This week, the weather report again shows little snowflake icons as far as NOAA can forsee, with 5"-9" expected before today is over, and another significant storm on Tuesday night.  Yesterday, I rode through heavy snowfall and found  first tracks off the Gondola and Thunder, carving my way through trees and gullies galore.  Temperatures have remained warm, but are expected to return to the teens and twenties after today.  See you on the slopes! -AC

It's National Safety Awareness Week!  Today's message:  Stay in control - people ahead of you have the right of way.
