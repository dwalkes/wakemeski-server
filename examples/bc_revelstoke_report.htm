Discussion of Snowpack and Stability: Welcome to another Revy Powder Day!!! Little change in the area snowpack in the last 24 hours with a little new snow last night and power flurries to keep the tracks filled all day yesterday. Avalanche Control using explosives produced a few larger avalanches within the storm snow at ridge top on Northerly aspects yesterday morning. Things should change drastically tonight as a strong system pushes in to the forecast area bringing at least 30mm of precipitation and strong SW to W winds tomorrow. Sounds like a good ole' fashion Revy Dump. Enjoy!!
