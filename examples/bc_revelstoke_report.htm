The newly-installed state of the art weather system, located at the Ripper Chair weather plot, provides accurate snowfall and weather data with automated updates to the RMR website every half an hour.

New snowfall figures are automatically updated at 6am and 6pm and refer to the previous 12 hours. Our goal is to provide you with the most accurate information possible so that you will be confident in your decision to ski/snowboard at Revelstoke Mountain Resort.
