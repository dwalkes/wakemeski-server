Synopsis
A broad low over the eastern pacific is moving much milder air over Whistler from the south. A small disturbance will bring some cloud with only light precipitation.

Read More »

On Monday the low will move a second disturbance over the area with cloud but only light precipitation late in the day.

A series of disturbances embedded in the flow will then give snow, heavy at times, and wind to the area Tuesday through Thursday.
